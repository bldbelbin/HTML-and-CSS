Nintendo Entertainment System (NES)

The NES will change the way you experience gaming. This 8-bit front-loading system allows for a 72-pin cartrige giving users an optimal gaming experience. Packed with every console is two controllers and the incredibly accurate NES Zapper. For a better experience play with R.O.B the extrodinary video robot, who is there to help you with your most challenging adventures.

NES Technical Specifications

  • CPU type: Motorola 6502 8 bit (MOS)
  • CPU speed: 1.79 MHz
  • RAM: 16 Kbit (2 Kbyte)
  • Video RAM: 16 Kbit (2 Kbyte)
  • Picture resolution: 256 x 240 pixels
  • Colors Available: 52 colors
  • Max colors at once: 24 colors
  • Max sprite size: 8 x 8 pixels or 8 x 16 pixels
  • Max sprites: 64 sprites (max per line: 8)
  • Min/Max Cart Size: 192 Kbit - 4 Mbit
  • Sound: PSG sound
  • Picture Scroll: 2 h.v
